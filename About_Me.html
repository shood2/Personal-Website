About Me

Sadie Hood is a current student at Northwestern university from a small town in Eastern Maine. She is a current junior majoring in Computer Science in Weinberg School of Arts and Sciences with a minor in Middle East North African Studies. She is also a transfer student; her first year of college she attended Illniois Tech, a small technical school in Bronzeville, Chicago.

Sadie is interested in how Computer Science can be used to aid the accessibility of quality education for people around the globe. She is also interested in the study and application of Natural Language Understanding and how that can be used in the field of education as well, particularly when focused on language learning and teaching. She believes that Computer Science can be used to lessen the divide in quality and accessibility of education in poorer areas in addition to the dramatic lack of funding for education in many areas.

Outside of her technical interests, she is heavily involved in different volunteering organizations on campus. She is a member and repeat Pledge Training Assistant of Alpha Phi Omega, the service fraternatity on Northwestern's Campus. In addition to that, she is invovled in Alternative student Breaks, a volunteer organization that serves to provide non-profits around the country with teams of volunteers from Northwestern University during the school's short breaks. She serves as one of the PR individuals on the executive board. She is also involved in her University's Habitat for Humanity group. She's participated in two collegiate challenges for Habitat for Humanity.

Outside of her volunteering organizations, she is involved in Northwestern's Women in Computing Chapter, and organization seeking to empower women, trans, and non-binary students, in the technical field. At her previous university, she was heavily involved in their similar organization, Association of Computing Machinery for Women. At this university, she helped plan and execute a Cybersecurity event that had attendees from three different universities. In addition to this, she enjoys to dance in her spare time and takes Latin and Ballroom dance lessons through a school organization and attends Contra Dances hosted by the Chicago Barn dance company.

If you have any questions for Sadie, she can be contacted at sadiehood2020@u.northwestern.edu
